Shred It.

Isometric Illustration

Shred It came alive while I was sitting downtown Ottawa, Ontario doodling in my sketchbook. I drew an eyeball and started drawing whatever came in my head around it. It turned out to be some sort of science fiction skatepark. This piece in specific truly taught me that you must always sketch before jumping in digitally.
