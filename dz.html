Danger Zone

Quote Book

Danger Zone was a fun project in which we had to choose a topic and mix illustration with typography. I chose a topic that would go hand in hand with my stupid sense of humour. This whole book consists of quotes from the TV show Archer, matched with an illustrated word to give the viewer a sense of what the show is all about.
